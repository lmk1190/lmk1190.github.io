Deborah Roth

The Entire PR Package

  • Deborah Roth has two decades of experience in developing and implementing comprehensive communications programs that drive companies’ evolution as disruptive industry leaders. Her extensive background in navigating companies through and beyond major inflection points such as IPOs and other material events stem from her innovative and game-changing approaches that elevate brand awareness to achieve overall business goals.

    Core competencies include: corporate communications, public relations, strategic communications, crisis communications, strategic partnerships, and leadership. Most recently Deborah held the position of Chief Communications Officer for OpenX, the world's leading independent advertising exchange. Deborah earned her B.A. in British and American Literature from Brandeis University.

Deborah's PR Process

A Proven Method

Foundational

I come on board to build the foundation: distill the narrative, create a messaging platform, design a short (12 - 18 month) and long (3 - 5 year) communications plan to achieve business goals.

Execution

Reassure test the plan, put it into motion while working with the in-house communications person build and staff a department of executors or selecting external PR agencies to support PR objectives.

Advisory

Retainer based where I’m kept in the loop, maybe do 1 - 2 executions a month, and remain available to be pulled in to consult or take on additional project work.
